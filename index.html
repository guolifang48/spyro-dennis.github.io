About Me

"First solve the problem. Then write the code."

This quote from John Johnson is how I approach web development. My goal is always to understand your needs and objectives and to carefully consider what solution will work best for you and your customers. Then I write the code to implement that solution.

My expertise is in PHP(Laravel Framework) and React, with much of my experience focused on customizing Shopify and WordPress. Over the past six years I have developed a wide range of projects from marketing and e-commerce to process automation and scientific research tools. I am equally capable of writing server-side code, managing MySQL databases, or developing the front end of a website. My toolset includes popular frameworks like Boostrap and Javascript libraries like jQuery, Vue.js and Angular.

In addition to writing code, I am also experienced in Linux server administration, Apache configuration, domain name and DNS migration, and the multitude of other tasks that go along with managing a website.
